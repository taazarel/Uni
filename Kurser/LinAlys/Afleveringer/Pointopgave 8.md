## a
For en matrix $X=[x_{ij}]\in \mathbb{M}_{2,2}$ definerer vi $\langle A,B \rangle=$
$$\left[
\begin{array}{cc}
1 & 2 \\ 0 & 7
\end{array}
\right]
\left[
\begin{array}{cc}
3 & 4 \\ 0 & 5
\end{array}
\right] =\left[
\begin{array}{cc}
3 & 14 \\ 0 & 35
\end{array}
\right] $$
Vi finder sporet til denne matrice
$$Tr(A^{t}B)=Tr\left[
\begin{array}{cc}
3 & 14 \\ 0 & 35
\end{array}
\right] =3+35=38$$
